Privacy Policy

Effective: March 25, 2026

ClairTools builds privacy-first Android apps. This policy covers all ClairTools apps: Prompter, Vault, and MoveProof.

What we don't do

  • No ads We never display ads. No ad SDKs. No ad tracking.
  • No accounts We never require sign-up, login, or email.
  • No data selling We never sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.
  • No cloud uploads Your files stay on your device unless you explicitly share them.
  • No tracking We don't track your location, contacts, messages, or browsing.

What we collect

Prompter & MoveProof (Firebase Analytics)

  • Crash reports — via Firebase Crashlytics, to fix bugs. Contains device model, OS version, and stack trace. No personal data.
  • Basic usage analytics — via Firebase Analytics with all ad-related consent denied (adStorage=DENIED, adPersonalization=DENIED, adUserData=DENIED). We see aggregate counts (e.g., "50 users opened settings"), never individual behavior.
  • Firebase Installation ID — an anonymous device identifier used by Firebase. It is not an advertising ID and cannot identify you.

Vault

  • No analytics. No Firebase. No crash reporting. Vault is intentionally offline-only. The only network permission is for in-app purchase processing via Google Play.

All apps (RevenueCat billing)

  • Purchase history — processed by RevenueCat to manage one-time in-app purchases. RevenueCat receives a pseudonymous app user ID and purchase receipts from Google Play. No name, email, or payment details are sent to us.

Data storage

  • All user data (scripts, photos, notes, encrypted files) is stored locally on your device.
  • Vault files are encrypted with AES-256-GCM using keys stored in the Android Keystore. Even if someone accesses the raw files, they cannot read them without your PIN.
  • Backups are created as local ZIP files that you control. Nothing is uploaded.

Permissions

  • Camera — Prompter: recording. MoveProof: inspection photos. Vault: importing photos.
  • Microphone — Prompter: video recording audio. MoveProof: optional video notes.
  • Location — MoveProof only: GPS coordinates on inspection photos (optional, for proof).
  • Internet — Required for in-app purchases (RevenueCat/Google Play) and AI generation (Prompter/MoveProof, user-configured). Never used to upload your data.
  • Bluetooth — Prompter only: remote control for teleprompter scrolling.

Your rights

  • Access — All your data is on your device. You can see it anytime.
  • Deletion — Delete any item in-app, or uninstall the app to remove everything.
  • Portability — Use the backup/export feature to get your data as a ZIP file.
  • No account = no server data — Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to request deletion of on our servers.

Children

ClairTools apps are general-audience tools. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data and require no accounts, there is no age-gated content.

Changes

If we change this policy, we'll update this page and the effective date above. Since we have no accounts or emails, we can't notify you directly — check this page periodically.
